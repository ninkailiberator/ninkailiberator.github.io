Data Protection and Deletion Guide

1. Introduction

At StarlinGame, we’re committed to ensuring the integrity, availability, and confidentiality of your data when you play "Ninkai Liberator". This guide is designed to provide information on how we protect your data and how you can request its deletion.

2. Data Protection

a. Storage Security:

All data we collect is stored in secure, encrypted databases, protected against unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

b. Data Transmission:

Any data transmitted between your device and our servers is done over encrypted channels, ensuring its security during transfer.

c. Third-party Services:

For third-party services like Firebase and AppsFlyer, we ensure that these services are compliant with the highest industry standards of data protection.

d. Access Control:

Only authorized personnel have access to our data storage solutions, ensuring your data isn’t accessed improperly.

3. Data Deletion

a. User-Requested Deletion:

As per Google’s policies and in compliance with relevant laws, users have the right to request the deletion of their data.

  • Requesting Deletion: Users can request the deletion of their data by sending an email to genjutsuwarrior@gmail.com. Please provide sufficient identification information so we can validate your identity and process the deletion.

  • Processing Time: Upon receiving a valid request, we will process the deletion within 30 days.

  • Data Backup: It’s important to note that while immediate data deletion will occur in our primary storage, backups may retain the data for a short period after the deletion request. However, this data will not be accessible for regular use and will be permanently deleted in subsequent backup cycles.

b. Automatic Deletion:

Inactive accounts or data that haven’t been accessed or used for an extended period might be automatically deleted as part of our regular data maintenance protocol.

4. Conclusion

Your data’s security is our utmost priority. We strive to provide transparent mechanisms for data protection and deletion in line with Google’s policies. For any further queries or concerns related to data, feel free to reach out to us at genjutsuwarrior@gmail.com.
